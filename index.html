        Getting Started & Installation ▼

        What is True Sky?

        True Sky is an online astrology software app that creates astrological charts that help you understand planetary influences in yours or someone elses life. It can be accessed by all devices, anywhere there is an internet connection.

        What Can You Do Here?

        • Create Birth Charts: Calculate your natal chart using your birth date, time, and location
        • Track Planetary Influences: See how transiting planets affect your birth chart
        • Analyze Relationships: Compare charts with partners, friends, or family
        • Join Community: Connect with other astrology enthusiasts

        Install True Sky App (Recommended)

        Install True Sky for quick access on any device, including desktop. Look for the install icon in your browser's address bar above, or:

        • Chrome (Recommended): Menu (⋮) → Save and share → Install app
        • Edge: Menu (⋯) → Apps → Install this site as an app
        • Safari iOS: Share button → Add to Home Screen → Add
        • Safari Mac: File → Add to Dock (macOS Sonoma or later)

        Alternative: Bookmark True Sky using Ctrl+D (Windows/Linux), Cmd+D (Mac), or your browser's bookmark menu. We recommend using Google Chrome for optimal performance and compatibility.

        Setting Up True Sidereal (Optional)

        To use the True Sidereal zodiac system, which aligns with the actual star constellations:

        1. Navigate to the SETTINGS section
        2. Set Zodiac to "Midpoint (True Sidereal)"
        3. Set House to "Equal"
        4. Set Coordinates to "Topocentric"

        These settings provide calculations based on the actual astronomical positions of the constellations, rather than the traditional zodiac of 30-degree signs.

        Step 1: MAIN: Create Your First Chart ▼

        Your Natal Chart - The Foundation of Astrology

        Your birth chart (natal chart) is a precise map showing where all the planets were positioned at the exact moment and location of your birth. This is the cornerstone of all astrological readings and the starting point for every other feature in True Sky.

        Required Information

        • Birth Date: Day, Month, Year
        • Birth Time: Hour and minute in 24-hour format
        • Birth Location: City, region, country with automatic coordinate lookup
        • Chart Name: Personal identifier for your chart

        Creating Your First Chart

        1. Click MAIN: The first button in the top menu
        2. Name Field: Type any identifying name (your name, "My Chart", etc. - max 30 characters)
        3. Birth Date Fields:
          • Day: Type number 1-31
          • Month: Select from dropdown menu
          • Year: Type full year (e.g., 1985, 2001)
        4. Birth Time Fields:
          • Hour: 0-23 (14 = 2 PM, 20 = 8 PM)
          • Minute: 0-59
          • Unknown time? Use 12:00 (noon) as estimate
        5. Birth Location Field:
          • Start typing your birth city
          • Select from autocomplete suggestions
          • Coordinates automatically calculated from city selection
          • Custom locations can be entered by typing a unique location name and entering the coordinates followed by the checkmark ✔
        6. Click Calculate: Generates your natal chart data
        7. Click View: Displays your saveable and printable natal chart
        8. Share Charts: Generated charts, graphs, and reports can be freely shared for professional or personal use

        Understanding Your Chart Display

        • Chart Wheel: Circular diagram with 12 house sections
        • House Numbers: 1-12 sections representing life areas
        • Zodiac Signs: The 12 signs around the outer rim
        • Planet Positions: Symbols showing exact zodiac degrees
        • Aspect Lines: Colored lines connecting planets showing relationships between planets
        • Planet Degrees: Exact positions (if enabled in Settings)

        Chart Management Features

        • Click Save: Store charts permanently in your collection (max 5K)
        • Saved Charts List: View all your saved charts below the form
        • Search Function: Type to find specific charts quickly
        • Chart Selection: Select any saved chart name to instantly display it

        Interactive Chart Features

        • Chart Animation: Animate chart by hour, minute, day, month, or year for chart rectification
        • Interactive Planet Display: Click and examine different planets and points in your chart
        • Aspect Display: Click center of chart to bring up aspectarian
        • Aspect Interpretations: Click individual aspects within the aspectarian to view detailed interpretations
        • Click View Report: Get detailed astrological interpretation of your natal chart

        Common Chart Creation Issues

        • Chart already exists in database: Click Calculate before saving new charts
        • Location Not Found: Try larger city or alternate spelling
        • Time Zone Confusion: True Sky automatically handles time zones
        • Historical Dates: Works for any date in recorded history

        Step 2: TRANSIT: See Current Influences ▼

        What Are Transits?

        Transits show how the planets in the sky interact with your birth chart. This reveals what energies are active in your life at any given point in time.

        Creating a Transit Chart

        1. First: Make sure you've created your birth chart in the MAIN section
        2. Click TRANSIT: This is the second button in the top menu
        3. Date And Time Fields: Usually today's date, but you can pick any date
        4. Location Field: Where you are right now (only necessary for hourly forecasting)
        5. Click Calculate: Generates your transit chart data
        6. Click View: Displays your saveable and printable transit chart showing three rings of planets known as a triwheel

        Reading Your Triwheel

        You'll see three circles:

        • Inner Circle: Your birth chart (the reference chart for the triwheel)
        • Middle Circle: Your seconday progressed planets (slow evolution over years)
        • Outer Circle: Transiting planets in the sky

        Biwheel Modes

        Use the radio buttons below the chart to switch between viewing modes:

        • Prog & Tran (Default): Full triwheel showing natal, progressions, and transits
        • Progressions: Biwheel showing only natal (inner) and progressions (outer)
        • Transits: Biwheel showing only natal (inner) and transits (outer)

        What to Look For

        • Progressed Personal Planets (Moon to Mars): Shows your psychological and emotional development over a large period of time
        • Transiting Outer Planets (Jupiter to Pluto): Reflect major life themes and longer cycles unfolding across one or more years
        • Transiting Inner Planets (Moon to Mars): Indicate day-to-day activity, daily motivations, and short-term changes

        Interactive Chart Features

        • Animate Chart Option: Animate by year, month, day, hour, or minute below the chart
        • Interactive Planet Display: Click and examine different planets and points across all three wheels in your triwheel
        • Aspect Display: Click center of chart to bring up aspectarian
        • Aspect Interpretations: Click individual aspects within the aspectarian to view detailed interpretations
        • Click View Report: Get detailed astrological interpretation of your transit chart

        Step 3: GRAPH: Plan Ahead with Graphs ▼

        Interactive Timeline for Planetary Timing

        The Graph section creates an interactive timeline showing when planetary influences will be strongest during certain periods of time. Perfect for timing important astrological events and tracking planetary cycles.

        Creating Your Timing Graph

        1. Click GRAPH: The third button in the top menu
        2. Set Start Date: Enter the beginning date for your timeline
        3. Set End Date: Enter the ending date (recommended 2-3 years)
        4. Enter Location: Where you'll be during this period (only necessary for hourly forecasting)
        5. Click Calculate: Generate your timing graph
        6. Click View: Display your interactive timeline in saveable and printable form

        Date Range Limits

        To ensure efficient calculations, date ranges are limited based on your planet selection in SETTINGS:

        • Standard Limits:
          • Maximum range: 5 years
          • With fast transits (Sun–Mars): 3 months
          • With transiting Moon: 1 month
        • Extended Ranges: Select only one transiting planet with no progressed planets to use longer ranges:
          • Single outer planet (Jupiter–North Node): up to 20 years
          • Single fast planet (Sun–Mars): up to 2 years
          • Moon only: up to 6 months

        Interactive Features

        The graph offers multiple ways to interact with your timeline:

        • Triwheel Preview: Shows detailed planetary influences at that specific moment
          Desktop: Left-click on graph | Mobile: Tap on graph
        • Move Timeline Marker: Slides the red timeline marker to different dates
          Desktop: Right-click and drag | Mobile: Tap and drag
        • Aspect Interpretations: Click aspect labels on the left side of the graph to view detailed interpretations
        • Click View Report: Get detailed astrological interpretation of your graph timeline

        Step 4: RETURN: Annual And Monthly Forecasting ▼

        Your Yearly and Monthly Themes

        Return charts show the overall themes and energy patterns for entire years (Solar Return) or months (Lunar Return) below the chart. Perfect for understanding the major astrological themes during a specific year or month.

        Creating Return Charts

        1. Click RETURN: This is the fourth button in the top menu
        2. Name Field: Enter a name to identify this return chart
        3. Automatic Date/Time: The date and time fields are automatically calculated from the natal chart and cannot be edited
        4. Location Field: Defaults to the natal location but can be changed to current location if preferred
        5. Click Calculate: Generate the return chart data
        6. Click View: Display your return chart wheel
        7. Change Return Type: Select Solar or Lunar return below chart
        8. Past And Future Return: Use the arrows ← → under the chart to change between past and future returns

        How Return Charts Work

        • Solar Return: Calculated for when the Sun returns to your exact birth position (annually)
        • Lunar Return: Calculated for when the Moon returns to your exact birth position (monthly)
        • Automatic Calculation: True Sky determines the exact date and time automatically
        • Location Matters: You can change the location to see how being elsewhere affects your return
        • Yearly And Montly Themes: Shows the overarching energy and themes for that year or month

        Interactive Chart Features

        • Interactive Planet Display: Click and examine different planets and points in your return chart
        • Aspect Display: Click center of chart to bring up aspectarian
        • Aspect Interpretations: Click individual aspects within the aspectarian to view detailed interpretations
        • Click View Report: Get detailed astrological interpretation of your return chart

        Understanding Your Connections

        Synastry charts compare two people's birth charts to reveal relationship dynamics, compatibility, and areas of harmony or challenge. Perfect for romantic relationships, friendships, business partnerships, or family connections.

        4. Click Calculate: Generate the synastry chart data
        5. Click View: Display the saveable and printable synastry chart wheel
        6. Click Save: Store the second person's chart data for future reference

        Reading Your Synastry Chart

        • Inner Circle: Your natal chart (from MAIN section
        • Outer Circle: The second person's birth chart
        • Colored Lines: Planetary aspects between the two charts (can be clicked to view aspectarian)
        • Swap Charts: Charts can be swapped below the synastry wheel to show how one chart relates to the other

        What to Look For

        • Harmonious Aspects: Often shown in blue - areas of natural compatibility
        • Challenging Aspects: Often shown in red - areas requiring understanding
        • Planets Close Together: Shared values and similar approaches

        Your Relationship as One Entity

        Composite charts create a single chart representing your relationship itself - not how you interact individually, but what your partnership creates together. Perfect for understanding the unique energy and purpose of your relationship.

        Creating a Composite Chart

        1. Click COMPOSITE: This is the second button on the bottom row in the top menu

        Note: Composite charts are automatically generated from existing natal chart data. The form only contains a View button since no additional input is needed.

        How Composite Charts Work

        The composite chart uses mathematical midpoints between your planets:

        • Midpoint Planets: Each planet position is the midpoint between both of your planets
        • Relationship Personality: Shows the character and energy of your partnership
        • Shared Purpose: Reveals what your relationship is meant to accomplish

        Understanding Your Composite

        • Sun Sign: The core identity and purpose of your relationship
        • Moon Sign: The emotional nature and needs of your partnership
        • Rising Sign: What your relationship is destined to develop
        • Planetary Aspects: The internal dynamics of your partnership

        Technical Specifications ▼

        Planetary Position Accuracy

        The accuracy of planetary positions varies based on the time period and celestial body:

        Major Planets (Sun through Pluto)

        • Available Range: 10,000 BCE to 10,000 CE
        • Highest Accuracy: 3000 BCE to 3000 CE
        • Note: Positions are reliable throughout the entire range, with maximum precision during the central 6000-year period

        Asteroids (Ceres, Vesta, Pallas, Juno, and custom points)

        • Most Accurate: 1800 CE to 2400 CE
        • Note: Precision decreases outside this range due to limited observational data and orbital perturbations

        Chiron

        • Available Range: 700 CE to 4650 CE only
        • Automatic Exclusion: Chiron is automatically removed from calculations outside this period due to orbital instability
        • Note: Chiron's highly eccentric orbit makes long-term predictions unreliable beyond these dates

        Calculated Points

        • Lunar Nodes: Calculated using True or Mean Node method (defaults to True)
        • Black Moon Lilith & Priapus: Calculated using True or Mean lunar apogee (defaults to Mean)
        • Vertex, Anti-vertex, Part of Fortune, and Part of Spirit: Standard mathematical calculations
        • Highest Accuracy: 3000 BCE to 3000 CE
        • Note: These mathematical points are available across all dates but with optimal precision in the central time period

        Calculation Methods

        Some astrological calculations employ specific methodologies that may differ from other standard practices:

        Progressions

        • Method: Secondary Progressions (day per year)
        • Midheaven: Solar Arc longitude

        True Sidereal (Midpoint Method)

        • Boundaries: Midpoints of constellations along the ecliptic
        • Constellations: 13 signs including Ophiuchus
        • Sign Sizes: Variable based on actual constellation sizes
